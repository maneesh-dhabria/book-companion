Behavior summary. Empty state shows the dropzone alone. On file pick: dropzone dims and a per-file card appears with a determinate progress bar and live byte count; Cancel aborts the HTTP request. When upload reaches 100%, the bar is replaced by an indeterminate spinner with "Parsing EPUB…"; Cancel becomes disabled. On success the wizard auto-advances to Step 2; on failure the error banner appears with a Retry CTA.
