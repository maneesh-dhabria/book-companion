Try these journeys

  1. Open the desktop prototype, click Thinking, Fast and Slow in the sidebar, navigate to a section, press Play. Hit Space to pause. Watch the sentence highlight advance.
  2. Go to the Audio tab on Atomic Habits, click Generate audio. Confirm the modal — watch the job animate through the in-memory store.
  3. Open Thinking, Fast and Slow → Annotations tab → Play as audio. Notice the cue divider between highlight and note.
  4. On Deep Work, click the stale-source banner's Regenerate CTA to clear stale audio.
  5. Settings → TTS: sample both Kokoro and Web Speech voices. Read the spike summary.
  6. From any audio-rich book, hit the Delete audio button on the Audio tab — note the type-to-confirm gate.
