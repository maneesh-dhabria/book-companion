Upload & Processing — Summary

  • 3-step wizard: Upload (drag-drop) → Metadata (auto-extracted, editable, tag assignment) → Review Structure (section list with quality warnings + suggested actions)
  • Quality warnings: Inline on the section list — stub sections, empty content, encoding issues. Suggested actions: Merge, Delete, Keep.
  • Section editing: Drag to reorder, ⋯ menu for merge/split/delete/change type. Can also edit later from Book Detail.
  • Two exit paths: "Save without processing" (parse only) or "Confirm & Summarize" (opens preset picker).
  • Preset picker: Select system or user preset, or configure custom facets. Options for eval and auto-retry.
  • Progress tracking: Pinned card in Book Detail showing section-by-section progress with eval scores, timing, and estimated remaining. Completed sections immediately browsable. SSE-driven updates.
