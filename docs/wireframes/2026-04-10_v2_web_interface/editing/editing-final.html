Newport argues that deep work is becoming increasingly rare precisely as it becomes increasingly valuable. The ability to concentrate without distraction on a cognitively demanding task is a skill that:

  • Allows you to quickly master hard things
  • Enables production of output at an elite level, in terms of both quality and speed
  • Creates meaning and a sense of craftmanship in work|

The core formula presented: High-Quality Work = Time Spent x Intensity of Focus
